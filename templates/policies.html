Privacy Policy & Terms

We are committed to protecting your data and being transparent about how we use it.

1. Data Collection

We collect personal information (name, email, phone, CV) solely for facilitating job applications and matching with suitable employers. All data is collected with your consent.

2. Data Use

Your data is shared only with employers for recruitment purposes and is never sold to third parties. We use it to improve job matching and send relevant notifications.

3. Cookies

We use session cookies to keep you logged in. No advertising or tracking cookies are used. You can disable cookies in your browser settings.

4. File Uploads

Uploaded CVs and documents are stored securely and accessible only to authorised employers reviewing applications you have submitted.

5. Security

All passwords are hashed using bcrypt. Connections are protected with CSRF tokens. We follow industry-standard security practices.

6. Contact & Data Removal

For data removal requests or privacy concerns, contact us via the Contact page. We will respond within 48 hours.
